Layne Staley

  1. 1967 - Born in Seattle, Washington; the birthplace of grunge music.
  2. 1987 - Forms Alice in Chains, a band that would go on to revolutionize the music industry with their unique blend of hard rock and heavy metal.
  3. 1990 - Signs with Columbia Records and releases their debut album, Dirt, which would go on to sell over 10 million copies worldwide.
  4. 1993 - Releases Facelift, a powerful and critically acclaimed album that cemented Alice in Chains' place as one of the most influential and beloved bands of the 1990s.
  5. 1996 - Releases Sap, an album that showed a maturity and depth in their sound that would be a defining feature of their career.
  6. 2006 - Releases Black Gives Way to Blue, a double album that would go on to sell over 10 million copies worldwide and cement Alice in Chains' place as one of the most successful bands of the 2000s.
  7. 2016 - Releases The Devil Put Dinosaurs Here, a powerful and emotionally charged album that would go on to be hailed as one of the best albums of the decade.
  8. 2002 - Sadly passes away from an overdose at the age of 48, leaving behind a legacy that would continue to inspire and influence generations of musicians to come.
